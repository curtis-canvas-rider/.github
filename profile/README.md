<h2>Purpose of Canvas Rider</h2>
<p>Canvas Rider is a browser-based game that allows players to ride a bicycle on 2D tracks drawn by other users. The goal is to provide an accessible, creative platform for physics-based bike riding and community-driven level sharing using only JavaScript and HTML5.</p>

<h2>Main Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Thousands of user-generated tracks to ride: from simple terrain to complex obstacle courses.</li>
  <li>Ability to design and submit your own tracks - you draw the course and then ride on it.</li>
  <li>Simple controls: arrow keys (pedal, brake, balance), making the game easy to start but difficult to master.</li>
  <li>Physics-based ragdoll-style bike and rider movement for realistic (or hilariously unrealistic) results.</li>
  <li>Runs directly in modern web browsers without additional downloads - purely HTML5 & JavaScript.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Unique Aspects</h2>
<p>Canvas Rider stands out by combining community creativity and physics-based gameplay in a lightweight browser format. The fact that tracks are drawn by players gives virtually unlimited variety and replayability. Also, being HTML5-based means it's accessible on most devices with a web browser, no installation required.</p>

<h2>Target Audience</h2>
<p>Canvas Rider appeals to casual gamers, retro-game enthusiasts, physics-game fans, and creative players who enjoy designing and sharing levels. It's also good for anyone looking for a quick and fun browser-based game without the friction of installs or heavy system requirements.</p>
